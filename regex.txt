Number,Date,"First Name","Last Name",City,State/Province,Country,"Why is this issue important to you?"

"^(\\d+),(\\d\\d-\\d\\d-\\d\\d\\d\\d),(\"[^\"]*\"|[^,]*),(\"[^\"]*\"|[^,]*),(\"[^\"]*\"|[^,]*),(\"[^\"]*\"|[^,]*),(\"[^\"]*\"|[^,]*),(\"[^\"]*\"|[^,]*)$"
